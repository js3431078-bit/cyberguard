🔄 How It Works
1

Register & Login

Create a free account with your name, email and phone number to get started.

2

Describe the Incident

Fill in the complaint form with details of the cybercrime you experienced.

3

AI Analysis

Our AI engine instantly analyzes your complaint and identifies the crime type and threat level.

4

Upload Evidence

Attach screenshots, images or videos as evidence to strengthen your complaint.

5

Submit Complaint

Submit your complaint securely. It is forwarded to the relevant cyber crime authority.

6

Track & Resolve

Monitor your complaint status through your dashboard until it is resolved.
