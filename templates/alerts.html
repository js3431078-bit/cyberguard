🚨 Cyber Alerts & Threat Updates

Latest cybercrime threats, scams, and safety advisories for students in India

🔴 High Priority Alerts
🎣

Fake College Portal Phishing Campaign

Fraudsters are sending emails mimicking official college portals asking students to "verify" their login credentials. These are fake — never click such links.

🔴 High Risk Phishing Students April 2026
💸

Fake Scholarship / Part-Time Job Scams

Scammers are targeting college students with fake scholarship offers and part-time job opportunities requiring upfront "registration fees". Do not pay.

🔴 High Risk Financial Fraud Students March 2026
📱

WhatsApp OTP Hijacking

Attackers are calling students pretending to be friends and asking for WhatsApp OTP codes to "help them log in". This transfers control of your account to them.

🔴 High Risk Account Takeover March 2026
🟠 Medium Priority Alerts
🤖

AI-Generated Deepfake Sextortion

Criminals are using AI to create morphed images from social media profiles and threatening victims. Report immediately to cybercrime.gov.in — do not pay.

🟠 Medium Sextortion Social Media February 2026
📦

Fake Delivery SMS Scams

Fake SMS messages claiming "your package is held" with a link to pay customs fees. These links steal your card details. Verify directly with the courier company.

🟠 Medium Smishing February 2026
🎮

Gaming Account Credential Theft

Fake "free coins" and "premium account" websites are stealing gaming credentials. Never enter your login on third-party sites.

🟣 Low-Medium Gaming Students January 2026
🟢 Safety Tips
🔐

Enable 2FA on All Accounts Now

Two-Factor Authentication prevents 99% of account takeover attacks. Enable it on Gmail, Instagram, WhatsApp, and your college portal immediately.
