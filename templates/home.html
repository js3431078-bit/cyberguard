🔒 Student Cyber Safety Tips

🎣 Phishing on College Portals

Fake login pages mimicking your college portal steal your credentials. Always check the URL before logging in.

😡 Cyber Bullying & Harassment

Online bullying via WhatsApp groups, Instagram or Discord is a crime. Screenshot and report immediately.

💸 Fake Scholarship / Job Scams

Never pay fees for "guaranteed" scholarships or internships. Verify through official college channels only.

🔐 Social Media Safety

Keep your profiles private. Never share your location, college ID or personal details with strangers online.

🦠 Malware & Ransomware

Never download files from unknown sources. Keep your antivirus updated and avoid pirated software.
