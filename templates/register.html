CyberGuard Portal — Terms of Use

1. Acceptance: By registering, you agree to use this portal only for legitimate cybercrime reporting purposes.

2. Accuracy: You agree to provide accurate and truthful information when filing complaints. False reports are a punishable offence under Indian law.

3. Privacy: Your personal data is stored securely and used only for complaint processing. It will not be shared with third parties except law enforcement agencies.

4. Confidentiality: Do not share your login credentials or OTP with anyone. CyberGuard will never ask for your password.

5. Misuse: Any misuse of this portal, including filing false complaints or attempting to hack the system, will result in account termination and legal action.

6. Jurisdiction: This portal operates under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and Indian Penal Code provisions related to cybercrime.

7. Helpline: For urgent cyber crime assistance, call 1930 or visit cybercrime.gov.in.
